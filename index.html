About FitZone Gym

We are more than just a gym - we are a fitness community dedicated to helping you achieve your goals.

Your Local Fitness Destination

FitZone Gym was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to make fitness accessible and enjoyable for everyone in our community. We believe that everyone deserves to feel strong, healthy, and confident in their own skin.

Our 10,000 sq. ft. facility is equipped with the latest cardio and strength training equipment, dedicated functional training areas, and clean, modern amenities.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced athlete, our certified trainers will create a personalized plan to help you reach your goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FitZone Gym

What are your gym timings?

Our gym is open 24/7 for Pro and Elite members. Basic members have access from 6 AM to 10 PM daily.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we offer personal training sessions with our certified trainers. Pro membership includes 4 sessions per month, and Elite includes 8 sessions.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Please bring a valid ID proof, workout clothes, shoes, a towel, and a water bottle. We provide lockers for your belongings.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 3-day free trial for new members. You can experience our facilities and meet our trainers before joining.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we provide free parking for both cars and two-wheelers. Our parking area is secure and monitored.
